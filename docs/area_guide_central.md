# Central Singapore Area Guide: Novena, Toa Payoh & Bishan

## Overview

Central Singapore offers the best connectivity and urban convenience, but at higher rental prices. These mature residential towns are ideal for SMU students, those interning in the CBD, or anyone who wants quick access to everything Singapore has to offer.

## Novena

### The Medical and Lifestyle Hub

Novena is a well-connected central neighbourhood known for its healthcare cluster and shopping options. It's one of the more upscale residential areas outside the CBD.

**Distance to Universities:**
- SMU: 10-15 minutes by MRT (North-South Line)
- NUS: 25-30 minutes by MRT
- NTU: 50-60 minutes by MRT

**Rental Prices:**
- HDB Common Room: $900-1,300/month
- HDB Master Room: $1,200-1,700/month
- Condo Room: $1,400-2,000/month
- Condo Whole Unit (2-bed): $3,500-5,000/month

**Key Amenities:**
- Novena Square and Velocity@Novena: shopping and dining
- United Square: family-friendly mall
- Balestier Road: famous food street with local restaurants
- Tan Tock Seng Hospital and HealthCity Novena: major medical hub

**MRT Station:** Novena (North-South Line)

**Best For:** SMU students, healthcare interns, those who want a central location with good food options.

## Toa Payoh

### The Affordable Central Option

Toa Payoh is Singapore's second oldest satellite town, offering a more affordable central living experience. It has strong local character with traditional markets, hawker centres, and a community feel that's hard to find elsewhere.

**Distance to Universities:**
- SMU: 15-20 minutes by MRT
- NUS: 30-35 minutes by MRT
- SUTD: 30-35 minutes by MRT

**Rental Prices:**
- HDB Common Room: $750-1,000/month
- HDB Master Room: $1,000-1,400/month
- HDB Whole Unit (3-room): $2,000-2,800/month
- Condo Room: $1,200-1,700/month (Trevista, Trellis Towers)

**Key Amenities:**
- Toa Payoh Hub: integrated complex with library, sports facilities, and government services
- Toa Payoh Lorong 1-8 Hawker Centres: some of Singapore's best hawker food
  - Famous stalls: 127 Lorong 1 Fish Soup, Sam Soon Oyster Omelette (Lorong 5), Ocean Curry (Lorong 4)
- Toa Payoh Town Park: one of Singapore's oldest town parks
- Toa Payoh Polyclinic: affordable healthcare

**MRT Stations:** Toa Payoh, Braddell (North-South Line), Caldecott (Circle Line)

**Best For:** Budget-conscious students who want a central location, food lovers, those seeking authentic local living.

## Bishan

### The Family-Friendly Education Hub

Bishan is one of Singapore's most sought-after residential areas, known for its excellent schools, green spaces, and dual MRT connectivity. It's slightly more expensive but offers a very comfortable living environment.

**Distance to Universities:**
- SMU: 20-25 minutes by MRT
- NUS: 30-35 minutes by MRT (Circle Line to Buona Vista, then bus)
- NTU: 50-60 minutes by MRT

**Rental Prices:**
- HDB Common Room: $800-1,100/month
- HDB Master Room: $1,100-1,500/month
- HDB Whole Unit (3-room): $2,200-3,000/month
- Condo Room: $1,300-1,800/month

**Key Amenities:**
- Junction 8: major shopping mall with supermarket, cinema, and dining
- Bishan-Ang Mo Kio Park: one of Singapore's largest urban parks
- Multiple hawker centres at Bishan Street 11 and Street 24
- Many top schools: Raffles Institution, Raffles Girls' School, Catholic High School

**MRT Station:** Bishan (North-South Line + Circle Line interchange)

**Best For:** Students who want excellent connectivity (NSL + CCL), green spaces, and a safe residential environment.

## Transport Connectivity

Central Singapore has the best public transport connectivity on the island:

- **North-South Line (Red):** All three areas are on this line, giving direct access to Orchard Road, City Hall, and Marina Bay
- **Circle Line (Orange):** Available at Bishan and Caldecott (near Toa Payoh), connects to Buona Vista, one-north, and Paya Lebar
- **Major Expressways:** CTE and PIE run through this area for easy island-wide road access

**Commute Times to Key Locations:**
- Orchard Road: 5-15 minutes by MRT from any of the three areas
- CBD/Raffles Place: 15-25 minutes by MRT
- Changi Airport: 35-50 minutes by MRT

## Local Food Highlights

Central Singapore is a food paradise, especially for hawker food lovers:

- **Toa Payoh Lorong 8 Market:** Fish soup, oyster omelette, curry rice
- **Balestier Road (near Novena):** Famous for bak kut teh, roast meat, and claypot rice
- **Bishan Street 11 Hawker Centre:** Chicken rice, nasi lemak, and local desserts
- **Old Airport Road Food Centre (nearby):** One of Singapore's most famous hawker centres

Average meal cost at hawker centres: $3-6 per meal.
