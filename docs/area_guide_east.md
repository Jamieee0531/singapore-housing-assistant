# East Singapore Area Guide: Tampines, Bedok & Pasir Ris

## Overview

East Singapore offers a relaxed suburban lifestyle with beaches, parks, and family-friendly amenities. Rental prices are generally more affordable than central areas, and the upcoming Cross Island Line will further improve connectivity. This area is ideal for students at SUTD, SIT, or those who prefer a quieter living environment.

## Tampines

### The Eastern Regional Centre

Tampines is a major residential and commercial hub in the east. It has excellent amenities, multiple MRT connections, and a good balance of affordability and convenience.

**Distance to Universities:**
- SUTD (Changi): 15-20 minutes by MRT
- NUS: 40-50 minutes by MRT
- SMU: 30-35 minutes by MRT (Downtown Line)
- SIT@NYP (Ang Mo Kio): 35-40 minutes by MRT

**Rental Prices:**
- HDB Common Room: $600-850/month
- HDB Master Room: $800-1,100/month
- HDB Whole Unit (3-room): $1,600-2,200/month
- Condo Room: $900-1,400/month

**Key Amenities:**
- Our Tampines Hub: integrated community hub with sports facilities, library, hawker centre, and retail
- Tampines Mall, Century Square, Tampines 1: three major shopping malls in one area
- Tampines Round Market and Food Centre: affordable local food
- Changi Business Park: nearby employment hub for internships

**MRT Stations:** Tampines (East-West Line + Downtown Line interchange)

**Best For:** SUTD students, interns at Changi Business Park, students wanting good amenities at affordable prices.

## Bedok

### The Established Eastern Suburb

Bedok is one of Singapore's oldest and most established residential estates. It offers affordable rentals, authentic local food, and easy access to East Coast Park.

**Distance to Universities:**
- SUTD: 20-25 minutes by MRT
- NUS: 35-40 minutes by MRT
- SMU: 25-30 minutes by MRT

**Rental Prices:**
- HDB Common Room: $550-800/month
- HDB Master Room: $750-1,050/month
- HDB Whole Unit (3-room): $1,500-2,100/month
- Condo Room: $800-1,200/month

**Key Amenities:**
- Bedok Mall: connected to Bedok MRT and bus interchange
- Bedok Interchange Hawker Centre: massive hawker centre with diverse food options
- Bedok Reservoir Park: jogging, cycling, and kayaking
- East Coast Park: 15-minute bus ride, great for cycling and beach activities

**MRT Stations:** Bedok (East-West Line), Bedok North (Downtown Line)

**Best For:** Budget-conscious students who want an established neighbourhood with easy beach access.

## Pasir Ris

### The Coastal Town

Pasir Ris is the easternmost MRT station on the East-West Line, offering a peaceful coastal environment with the most affordable rents in the east. It's ideal for those who value nature and don't mind a longer commute.

**Distance to Universities:**
- SUTD: 10-15 minutes by MRT
- NUS: 50-60 minutes by MRT
- SMU: 35-45 minutes by MRT

**Rental Prices:**
- HDB Common Room: $500-750/month
- HDB Master Room: $700-950/month
- HDB Whole Unit (3-room): $1,400-1,900/month
- Condo Room: $800-1,200/month (Coco Palms, Ripple Bay, D'Nest)

**Key Amenities:**
- White Sands Mall: shopping and dining
- Downtown East: entertainment, dining, and Wild Wild Wet water park
- Pasir Ris Park and Beach: mangroves, cycling paths, BBQ pits by the sea
- Pasir Ris Polyclinic and nearby Changi General Hospital

**MRT Station:** Pasir Ris (East-West Line terminus, future Cross Island Line interchange)

**Future Developments:** The Cross Island Line (opening progressively from 2030) will make Pasir Ris a major interchange station, significantly improving connectivity to other parts of Singapore.

**Best For:** SUTD students, nature lovers, those seeking the lowest rents in the east.

## Transport Connectivity

- **East-West Line (Green):** All three areas are on this line, running from Pasir Ris to Jurong East/Tuas Link
- **Downtown Line (Blue):** Available at Tampines and Bedok North, connects directly to Bugis, Chinatown, and Botanic Gardens
- **Cross Island Line (future):** Will connect Pasir Ris to Ang Mo Kio and Jurong, avoiding the need to go through the city centre
- **Thomson-East Coast Line:** Sungei Bedok station connects to the east coast corridor

**Getting to the City:**
- Tampines to City Hall: about 30 minutes by MRT
- Bedok to Raffles Place: about 25 minutes by MRT
- Pasir Ris to City Hall: about 40 minutes by MRT

## Lifestyle and Recreation

- **East Coast Park:** Singapore's most popular beach park â€” 15 km of coastline with cycling, rollerblading, BBQ pits, and seafood restaurants. Accessible by bus from Bedok and Tampines.
- **Pasir Ris Park:** Mangrove boardwalk, beach, and nature area. One of Singapore's most scenic parks.

- **Changi Beach Park:** Quiet beach on the northeast coast, great for sunset views.

## Food Highlights

- **Our Tampines Hub Hawker Centre:** Modern hawker centre with diverse cuisines
- **Bedok Interchange Hawker Centre:** One of the largest hawker centres in the east
- **Changi Village Hawker Centre:** Famous for nasi lemak and international food
- **East Coast Lagoon Food Village:** Seafood BBQ by the beach

Average meal cost at hawker centres: $3-5 per meal.
